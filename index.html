Welcome to the Walsenburg Craft Emporium

Discover a world of quality craft supplies...

We are the Walsenburg Crafts Emporium. A local crafts supply shop working with the community to support your craft interests. We plan to have supplies for all kinds of crafts from fiber to electronics. We are starting small, with yarn, knitting and crochet supplies, and will expand as we can. We hope to hold classes, and community craft afternoons soon.
